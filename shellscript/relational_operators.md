# Relational Operators for Numbers
- -eq: equal
- -ge: Greater or Equal
- -gt: Greather Than 
- -le: Less or Equal
- -lt: Less Than
- -ne: Not Equal

# Relational Operators for Strings
- =: equal
- >: Greater Than
- <: Less than
- !=: Not Equal
- n: string isnt empty?
- z: string is empty?
